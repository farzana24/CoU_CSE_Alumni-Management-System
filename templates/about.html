About Us

Welcome to the Alumni Management System. Our platform is dedicated to connecting alumni and fostering a strong community. Whether you're looking to reconnect with old friends, network with professionals, or stay updated on alumni events, we've got you covered. The alumni of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Comilla University have established a remarkable presence in various industries and academic institutions, both nationally and internationally. Over the years, our graduates have made significant contributions in diverse fields, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Software Development, and Research & Development (R&D).

Many of our esteemed alumni have secured positions in leading multinational corporations (MNCs) such as Microsoft, where they are playing key roles in innovation and technology development. Additionally, our graduates have been making impactful contributions in renowned software firms, including Brain Station 21, WellDev, and Samsung R&D, excelling in software engineering and technological advancements.

Beyond the corporate world, our alumni have also made their mark in academia. Many of them are serving as faculty members and researchers in prestigious universities across Bangladesh. Some notable institutions where our alumni are currently working include the Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST) in Cumilla, Comilla University, and Daffodil International University. Their dedication to research and education is shaping the future of computer science in Bangladesh.

Moreover, our alumni network extends beyond national borders. Many of our graduates are currently working and pursuing higher studies in countries like the United States, Japan, and Australia. We take immense pride in the achievements of our alumni and their continuous efforts to make a difference in the world of technology and education. Our alumni network remains a vital asset, fostering connections, mentorship, and collaboration to support current and future generations of students in their academic and professional journeys.
