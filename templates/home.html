Message from the Head of The Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Dear Alumni,

We are proud of the achievements and contributions of our alumni in various fields. Your success is a testament to the quality of education and training provided by our department. We encourage you to stay connected with us and participate in our events and initiatives. Our department has a long and distinguished history, dating back to 2009. We have produced many talented graduates who are now working in leading companies around the world. We are also committed to research, and our faculty members are actively publishing in top journals and conferences. I believe that our department has the potential to be one of the leading CSE departments in Bangladesh. With your hard work and dedication, I am confident that we can achieve great things together.

Best regards,

Mahmudul Hasan, SMIEEE
Head of The Department of Computer Science and Engineering
